Malaysia and it's Ever-growing Population

A visualization by Ethan Hor

Current Population of Malaysia (2020)

As the years go by, Malaysia and the world has shown a gradual increase in overall population. This visualization aims to give you information on how Malaysia's population has changed over the years, as well as how Malaysia's age group distribution is like. So without further adue, let us begin. The map above helps to show us the total population of each state in Malaysia (in the year 2020). States that are shaded with a darker shade of orange indicate a higher population and vice versa. A good example of this would be the state of Selangor. Shaded with the darkest shade of orange, it is currently the most populated state in Malaysia. For more information on each state, feel free to hover over the states on the map with your cursor.

Malaysia's change in population over the years 2010-2020

Now that we know how Malaysia's population is like in the year 2020, let us travel 10 years back in time, and observe how Malaysia's population has changed over 10 years. With the line chart on the left, we are able to observe Malaysia's population during 2010 was around 28208035 people. As the years went by, the total population rose to 32365999 in the year 2020. This shows an addition of 4157964 in the country, which can also be said to be a 14.74% increase in population over 10 years! If we continue to observe the line chart, we can also conclude that Malaysia's population has a gradual increase every year, which further reinforces the fact that Malaysia's population is ever-growing, and will continue to increase over the years to come.

Age Groups in Malaysia

We know that Malaysia's population is gradually increasing, but do we know how it's age group distribution is like? The pie chart on the left shows us the population of each age group in Malaysia. If we observe this pie chart, we can see that most Malaysians are around the age group of 18 years and above, with this age group covering 71.68% of the total country's population. It is also larger than the total population of the two other age groups combined! Coming in second is the age group of youngsters (aged below 18 years), the population of this age group covers 17.59% of the country's population. Finally, the age group with the least individuals are the age group of 60 years and above, with a total of 3502000 individuals, covering 10.72% of the country's population.

Age Groups in Malaysia (by state)

Some states in Malaysia are known to have more individuals in a certain age group. To help visualise this, we can use the interactive bar chart on the left! Select a desired age range from the dropdown table, and the bar chart will display the data for the age range that has been switched to.
